Event Handling  allows tasks and ISRs to communicate and synchronize with each other. By using Event Flags, you can create a robust and efficient system that responds quickly to external and internal events.
Alarms used to schedule and control the execution of tasks or functions at specific points in time. They are typically used for time-critical operations, such as periodic tasks, timeouts, or event-triggered operations. Alarms provide a way to trigger tasks based on the passage of time or specific events.
